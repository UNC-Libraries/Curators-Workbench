Arrangement

After capture and staging, a logical representation of the original files appears in the Arrangement section of the Project Navigation window. This hierarchy can be edited. Files the user may not want to appear in the end result (e.g. metadata files or file-system generated files) may be removed from this hierarchy. At the point of capture, the logical hierarchy mirrors that of the original folder structure, but you may choose to modify this arrangement and organize materials differently (e.g. one may wish to facilitate the discovery of files by end-users).

Additionally, item- and folder-level description may be added in the Arrangement section of the Project Navigation Window. Files and folders may be renamed. It is important to bear in mind that these changes only affect the logical representation of the files, not the originals themselves.
