Metadata Dictionaries

Dictionaries streamline the process of mapping your custom metadata to objects in crosswalks and forms. Dictionaries help you create crosswalks by conveniently packaging up your most common mappings and patterns for MODS elements. Dictionaries can be designed for all your most commonly used blocks of metadata and the crosswalk connections that are used to create them.

Dictionaries allows users and groups to share and reuse standard MODS patterns without having to build complex crosswalks for each project. Dictionaries include labels and descriptive text to guide their use. They can be stored on network drives and shared by teams.
